About

“Tunisian Campers” was founded in 2015 by “Mohamed Temimi”a young Tunisian who aims to promote
outdoor activities and sports in Tunisia such as Mountain sports, camping, hiking and trekking.
It offers many pallets of alternative tourism and conveys essential values such as the protection and preservation of the visited sites and places.

Work

The Tunisian Campers activities are mainly camping and trekking. The community also organizes attractive sports and environmental events for many participants.
Our values are universal and unavoidable :
  1. Respect for nature and others, in particular by protecting and preserving the visited sites.
  2. Solidarity through the sharing of social, cultural and human experiences.

Goals

We wish :
  • To encourage and connect nature enthusiasts and lovers.
  • To encourage and connect nature enthusiasts and lovers.To unite people to raise public awareness of environmental preservation by organizing large scale cleaning campaigns.
  • To reveal our country’s countless assets to more people.
  • To increase employability in remote regions affected by unemployment.
